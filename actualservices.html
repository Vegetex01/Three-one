Our Core Services

Tailored solutions for homes, offices,

Structured Cabling

We design and install structured cabling systems using high-performance CAT6, CAT6a, and CAT7 cables to support seamless communication, data transfer, and device integration. Whether you’re outfitting a new office, upgrading a commercial space, or wiring a multi-unit residential building, our solutions are clean, scalable, and engineered for longevity.

Fiber Optic Installations

CableCrew delivers top-tier fiber optic installations for enterprises, smart buildings, and telecom-grade networks. We handle everything from splicing and termination to performance testing using both single-mode and multi-mode fiber options to ensure fast, high-capacity, and future-proof performance.

EV Charging Point Wiring

As electric vehicles become the norm, homes, workplaces, and commercial lots need infrastructure to support them. Cable-x installs dedicated EV charger wiring that meets local codes, maximizes energy efficiency, and future-proofs your property for next-gen transportation.

Smart Office & IoT Integration

We outfit modern offices and facilities with the infrastructure needed for automation, security, and hybrid workflows. Our IoT-ready cabling supports everything from security cameras and access control systems to connected lighting, sensors, and monitoring devices—allowing your space to adapt in real time.

IT & Network Infrastructure

We help businesses and data centers build out intelligent IT environments with rack system design, power distribution, Wi-Fi deployment, and network redundancy. Every install is meticulously planned for airflow, cable management, and maintenance efficiency because infrastructure should never be an afterthought.
